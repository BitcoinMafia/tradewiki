What is this?



One does not simply find places to spend Bitcoin ...

This app visualizes the mountain of merchants listed on bitcoin.it/wiki/Trade , but sorts them based on their alexa ranks. Only merchants with ranks under 1,000,000 are shown. Data collected as of 28th Nov 2013.

Built on top of AngularJS and inspired by this. Source code on Github
